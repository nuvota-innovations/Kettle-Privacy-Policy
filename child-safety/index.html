Kettle Child Safety Standards

Our commitment to keeping Kettle a safe space for adults and enforcing strict policies against child exploitation.

📱 Kettle App 📅 Last Updated: March 2026 🌐 kettleweb.com
🛡️

Introduction

At Kettle, we are dedicated to providing a safe, professional, and secure platform for our users. We recognize the profound importance of child safety and have established rigorous standards to ensure our platform complies with global safety regulations, including Google Play's child safety standards policy.

🚫

Zero-Tolerance Policy

Kettle has a strict, absolute zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We do not permit any content, behavior, or activity that endangers, exploits, or sexualizes minors.

⚠️ Zero Tolerance: Any account found violating this policy, whether through sharing images, text, links, or attempting to groom minors, will be subject to immediate and permanent removal from the platform.

🔞

Age Requirement

The Kettle application is designed exclusively for adult professionals. We strictly enforce a minimum age requirement:

  • Our app is intended only for users aged 18 years or older.
  • If we discover that an account has been created by or belongs to an individual under the age of 18, it will be immediately suspended and deleted.
🚩

Reporting System

We rely on our community and our systems to maintain safety. Users can report abusive, illegal, or inappropriate content directly within the Kettle app.

ℹ️ To report content or users in the app, use the Report option available on every user's profile and within any specific chat or group. You can specify the reason for the report to help our team take swift action.

🔍

Moderation and Enforcement

Our moderation team actively reviews all reports submitted by users.

  • Reported content is prioritized and investigated promptly.
  • We will permanently ban any accounts involved in child exploitation or related violations without prior warning.
  • Offending content is immediately removed from the platform to prevent further dissemination.
🚓

Cooperation with Authorities

Kettle is fully committed to assisting in the fight against child exploitation globally.

  • In instances of suspected child abuse or exploitation, we cooperate extensively with law enforcement and relevant authorities (such as NCMEC or local equivalents).
  • We will preserve and provide necessary digital evidence, user data, and activity logs to authorities to aid in investigations and prosecutions, in accordance with applicable laws.
📬

Contact Information

If you have any safety concerns, need to report an emergency outside of the app, or have questions about our safety standards, please contact our Trust and Safety team immediately.
