How to Delete Your Account

If you no longer wish to use Kettle, you can permanently delete your account directly from the mobile application. This action will erase your profile, chat messages, and all associated personal data.

ℹ️ To delete your user account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Kettle mobile app on your device.
  2. Tap on the Profile section located in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Tap the Settings button (usually represented by a gear icon).
  4. In the Settings page, locate and tap the Delete Account option.
  5. A new screen will open displaying a warning about the deletion process.
  6. Read the warning, and then tap the "Delete My Account" button to confirm.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will not be able to recover your account or any related information.
